About The Challenge

Welcome to the Oman Uni Tech Challenge, an exciting opportunity for Omani university students to showcase their innovation and problem-solving skills on a global platform.


Organized by the Google Developer Students Club (GDSC) Sohar, this challenge brings together students, industry professionals, and academic leaders to address real-world problems through technology and collaboration.

The 17 Sustainable Development Goals of the United Nations

In 2015, the United Nations created 17 Sustainable Development Goals and aimed to achieve them by 2030. All 193 United Nations Member States agreed on these 17 goals to end poverty, ensure prosperity, and protect the planet.

For the 2024 Solution Challenge, your mission is to create a project that contributes to solving one or more of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals using Google technologies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Undergraduate students (18+ years).
  • Students from the collaborating universities (Sohar University, Arabic Open University, and UTAS Shinas).
  • Students from all faculties are eligible.

Guidelines

  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.
  • Participants can choose their team members based on mutual interests and skills.
  • Once registered as a group, teams cannot add a new member and can only remove one member.
  • Student must register to be participant in this challenge.

Idea Submission

  • Participants must submit project ideas before starting development.
  • Ideas must be original, without prior prototypes or research submissions elsewhere.
  • Submitted ideas require approval for participation.
  • Submission includes problem description, proposed solution, and expected impact.

Project Submission

  • Projects must be submitted by the specified deadlineby the grup leader.
  • Submissions should include a project report, code repository, and a presentation/demo video.
